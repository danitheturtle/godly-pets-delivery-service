A pretty fat duck with big flippers who waddles about like a penguin. Cute and cartooney.

Swims horizontally between platforms that are surface-level with water and lets the player ride it. Pretty much the only mechanic with face-to-face traversal so far

The twist is that in the air, the bird can do the same, but is too fat to go horizontal. It instead slowly glides downward, following the path of a staircase. Lets you "skip" past parts where you'd otherwise need a stair.
